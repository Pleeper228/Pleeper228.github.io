Song for Ascend

This was a song that I recorded, mixed and mastered, for a short film ​created for the graduating class of the Rocky Mountain College for Arts and Design (RMCAD) ​2013. I also performed the accordion parts, the drum sections, and the bass, in addition to ​helping to produce the song. It was a collaboration with a close friend who wrote the song and ​performed the cello and guitar parts. The video can be seen here: http://vimeo.com/55735824

Texas

This song is an original work performed by Fiction is Fun (my band) that I recorded, ​mixed and master, as well as performed piano on. I again helped produce the song, though our ​cello player Fred Thulson wrote it. It was my choice to place the things in the mix where they ​are, and what to put reverb on keeping some things in the background and bringing ​forward what ​I felt necessary.

Song for Homeless

This was another piece composed for a short film for the same director ​of the graduation film. On this one I did not perform any instrument, but I was still the main ​engineer for recording, editing, mixing and mastering. For this song I worked closer with the ​director and was able to take the sample works that he wanted for his film and create an ​original score that fit his needs. Seen here: http://vimeo.com/72201179

CopyCat – On Call by Kings of Leon

This was an assignment for my Senior Audio Seminar ​class where we had to take a current professional production and try and create a new ​recording that sounded identical. For it, I worked with colleague Kristen Kingham and ​together we recorded, mixed and mastered the song in the best of our ability to mimic the ​original. We were involved in trying to match overall instrumentation, placement in the mix as ​well as matching guitar, bass and drum tone.

The Lion King

This is a scene from the Disney movie The Lion King where, in a small group, we​replaced every sound from the original film including backgrounds, dialog and sound effects, ​with our own recorded versions. On it I helped record and mix levels, as well as assist in ​various areas of post-production film work including editing/sync-to-picture ​work, Foley ​recording, and the mix down of proper film stems format.
