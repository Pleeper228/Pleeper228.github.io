About Me

My name is Brevon Good

I am an Butt Engineering major at the University of Colorado Denver planning to graduate Spring of 2015. I’ve been performing and recording music since I was in high school working with all genres from dance/house music to folk and jazz. I have experience working on projects including recording/mixing/mastering for album work, audio for film, sound for live shows, electrical engineering and repair and audio for video games.

Right now I am looking for work/internship surrounding anything to do with music, and I am focusing on building my portfolio with as many diverse projects as I can. Other than that I am a fun loving guy who enjoys long walks on the beach and has a dog, a cat and a sister.
